Become a Founding State Outreach Ambassador

Help bring clear, balanced arthritis education to people in your community.

Not in high school? See adult volunteer roles below.

Help Build a Nationwide Student Network

Radiant Joint Alliance is creating a nationwide network of high school students who want to make arthritis education easier to find in their own communities. State Outreach Ambassadors connect local organizations with approved educational materials, help expand community outreach, and support the location directory.

No medical background is required. We provide the materials, outreach templates, and guidance.

Students from all 50 states are encouraged to apply. Multiple ambassadors may serve in each state.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

The program is intended for high school students in the United States who are interested in community service, healthcare, education, or public health. Students from every state are welcome.

Do I need medical experience?

No. Ambassadors use materials and outreach templates provided by Radiant Joint Alliance. They do not give medical advice or recommend treatment to individual patients.

How much time is required?

There is no fixed schedule. Students may choose activities that fit their availability and participate whenever your schedule allows. Every contribution makes a difference.

Can more than one student serve in the same state?

Yes. We expect most states to have several ambassadors, and students may work independently or coordinate local outreach together.

Can the hours count toward a school service requirement?

In many cases, yes. Ambassador activities are designed as meaningful community service through a registered nonprofit educational initiative. Because each school has its own policy, students should obtain approval before beginning. Radiant Joint Alliance can provide a service-hours letter or activity record confirming the work completed.

Not in High School? We Still Need You

Everything above is set up specifically for high school students, but plenty of what we do doesn't require a school ID. If you're a parent, a retiree, a nurse, or just someone who cares about this getting in front of more patients, there's a role here too.

Local Outreach

Share our site and location directory where you already spend time: a Nextdoor post, a local Facebook group, a senior center bulletin board, or a note to your own doctor's office.

Hospital Research

Help us track down LDRT programs that aren't in our directory yet, especially in states where we have few or no listings. Good fit if you don't mind some digging and a few phone calls.

Community Events

Represent Radiant Joint Alliance at health fairs, senior center visits, or community events in your area. Hand out flyers, answer basic questions, point people to the site.

Something Else

Translation, graphic design, social media, data entry, or something we haven't thought of. Tell us what you're good at and we'll find a way to use it.
