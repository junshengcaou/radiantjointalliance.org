What Donations Fund

Radiant Joint Alliance runs on volunteer time, but it isn't free to operate. Donations go directly toward:

  • Website hosting and domain costs
  • Printing flyers and educational materials
  • Postage and mailing costs for outreach to organizations
  • Supplies for in-person community events and health fairs
  • Social media outreach, including paid promotion to reach more patients

Make a Donation

Every dollar helps cover hosting, printing, and outreach, and gets this information in front of more patients who need it. Donations are handled securely through Venmo.
